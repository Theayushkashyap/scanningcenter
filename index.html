Vismaya Scanning Centre, located within Mudra Hospital in Tiptur, Karnataka, is a reputable and well-established diagnostic facility. Founded by Dr. Anil, a distinguished medical expert, Vismaya Scanning Centre is committed to providing high-quality and accurate scanning services to patients in the region.
Equipped with advanced scanning technology and operated by a team of skilled professionals, Vismaya Scanning Centre offers a comprehensive range of diagnostic imaging services. These services include ultrasound scans, X-rays, CT scans, MRI scans, and more. The center focuses on delivering precise and reliable results to aid in the diagnosis and treatment of various medical conditions.
